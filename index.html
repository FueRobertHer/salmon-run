Salmon Run

How to play

Tips

  • space to start/resume playing
  • p or esc to pause game
  • current is faster at the surface
  • you can't move while airborne

Movement

  • w : up
  • a : left
  • s : down
  • d : right

Goals

  • Become the Biggest Fish Ever
  • Eat to grow
  • Get hurt and You Will Shrink
  • You have 60 Seconds
